Fret Visual: Interactive Guitar Fretboard & Chord Explorer

A professional music theory tool for guitarists, bassists, and extended range players (7, 8, and 9 strings).

Core Features

  • Fretboard Visualizer: Explore scales and modes on any guitar or bass.
  • 9-String Support: Optimized for extended range instruments and custom tunings.
  • Real-time Chord Identification: Identify custom voicings and geometric shapes instantly.
  • Theory-Aware Builder: Discover chords within specific scales and modes.
  • Save & Export: Keep your sessions and favorite riffs with full export capability.

Guitar Theory for the Modern Player

Fret Visual isn't just a static diagram; it's a dynamic engine that calculates intervals, ergonomics, and voicings on the fly, supporting musicians from standard guitar to the most complex custom instrument setups.
